Who Am I?

I am Lorenzo, a passionate game programmer with a deep love for gaming. Ever since I first discovered the world of game development, I have been fascinated by the intricate mechanics that bring virtual worlds to life. Beyond coding, I am an adventurous and reliable individual who enjoys traveling and exploring different cuisines.

Team working

My ability to work in large teams and my strong social skills allow me to collaborate effectively, making teamwork an engaging and productive experience.

How it all started...

My journey into game development began in 2022, when I decided to enroll at Event Horizon School in Milan for the Game Programming course. Initially, diving into object-oriented programming with C# and C++ was challenging, as it required a completely new way of thinking and problem-solving. However, through dedication and hard work, I gradually mastered these concepts and was able to develop the projects showcased on my homepage. I am constantly learning and staying updated on the latest advancements in programming and game development, ensuring that I keep up with the newest trends and technologies shaping the industry.
